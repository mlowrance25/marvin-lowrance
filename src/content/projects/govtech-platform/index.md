A proprietary platform built for public sector transportation agencies. Delivers ranked high-risk corridor identification, crash cost estimation (KABCO-weighted, USDOT values), infrastructure prioritization, and safety ROI justification packages that cities use to defend capital budgets to leadership.

Deployed for SFMTA (San Francisco) and the City of Minneapolis. Built on Python APIs, React frontend, and CDS-aligned ETL pipelines.

**Stack:** Python, React, PostgreSQL, Docker, Kubernetes, CDS/curbLR standards
